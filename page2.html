In this page we will look at lists - ordered and unordered

Ordered list:

  1. first item
  2. second item
  3. third item

as you can see the list is ordered - every item has a unique identifier.

Unordered list:

  • item 1
  • item 2
  • item 3

this list is unordered - the items are marked by a marker but it's the same for all items.
